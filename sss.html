Overview

The Social Security System (SSS) provides a variety of benefits including:

  • Retirement Pension
  • Disability Benefits
  • Death and Funeral Benefits
  • Sickness and Maternity Leave

Application Requirements

  • Valid Government ID
  • SSS Number
  • Proof of Contribution
